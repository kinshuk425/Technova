What is Cybersecurity?



Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks aim to steal information, damage systems, or gain unauthorized access.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing – Fake emails pretending to be trusted sources.
  • Malware – Viruses, worms, Trojans.
  • Ransomware – Hackers lock your files for money.
  • Man-in-the-Middle Attacks – Hackers intercept communication.
  • DDoS Attacks – Overloading a server with fake requests.

Cybersecurity Tools

Important tools used by cybersecurity professionals:

  • Kali Linux
  • Wireshark
  • Metasploit
  • Nmap
  • Firewalls & Intrusion Detection Systems

How to Stay Safe

  1. Use strong passwords
  2. Enable 2-factor authentication
  3. Don't click unknown links
  4. Keep software updated
  5. Use antivirus and firewalls

Future of Cybersecurity

With increasing digital use, cybersecurity jobs are rapidly growing. Cloud security, ethical hacking, and AI-based security systems will dominate the future.
