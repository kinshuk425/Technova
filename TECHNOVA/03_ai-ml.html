What is Artificial Intelligence?



Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. AI systems can perform tasks such as decision making, problem solving, pattern recognition, and data analysis.

Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that enables computers to learn from data. Instead of being explicitly programmed, ML models find patterns and improve over time.

Types of Machine Learning

  • Supervised Learning – Learning from labeled data.
  • Unsupervised Learning – Finding patterns in unlabeled data.
  • Reinforcement Learning – Learning by trial and error.

Real-World AI Applications

  • Face Recognition (Phones, CCTV)
  • Recommendation Systems (Netflix, YouTube)
  • Chatbots & Virtual Assistants
  • Medical Diagnosis
  • Self-Driving Cars

AI has become a part of daily life and is transforming industries such as healthcare, finance, retail, and cybersecurity.

Neural Networks

Neural networks are inspired by the human brain. They consist of layers that process information at different levels, enabling tasks like image recognition, language translation, and speech processing.

Conclusion

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning play a crucial role in the future of technology. Learning AI allows you to build smart systems that can learn, adapt, and improve.
